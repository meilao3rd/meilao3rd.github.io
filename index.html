About Us

At sympawthy.org, our hearts beat for the well-being of animals. We are a 100% nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting local animal shelters through creative and heartfelt endeavors. By harnessing the passion and talents of young artists, we craft and sell handmade products, channeling every penny earned into caring for less fortunate animals. Our community-focused approach not only aids animals in need but also nurtures a spirit of generosity and creativity in the youth who participate. Join us in our mission to create, collect, share, and care—because together, we can make a pawsitive difference!
